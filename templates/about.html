About Our Platform

Production Analyser is a comprehensive web application designed to provide detailed analytics and insights into production processes. Our platform empowers manufacturers and production managers with actionable intelligence to optimize operations and drive efficiency.

This tool helps monitor and analyze key performance indicators such as cycle times, fault delays, and tip dress efficiency across various production lines, enabling data-driven decision making and continuous improvement.
